Welcome to The Chubby Cups. This comprehansive book catalog accesses exclusively free books for you to enjoy. Working off the idea that everyone should be given the opportunity to read free books located in the public domain, this search engine lets you find books copyright in a legitamite way without going through shady websites or outright piracy

Search for a book for school or for pleasure. For reference, most books published before 1923 are in the public domain. Please use the search function in the other tab or take a look through our suggested titles here in the carousel that contain the most popular reads.

As an additional feature, you can also access notes to attach to your books. Now you have convient place to both find free books and keep track of your thoughts about them.
